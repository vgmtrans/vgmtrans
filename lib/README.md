# VGMTrans Dependencies

Dear packager,
thank you for your interest in VGMTrans!

We recognize that every distribution and packaging situation has its own preferences and details. In order to coexist peacefully, we stick to the "mind-your-business" model. Where sensible, we give you options to disable vendored dependencies and use system-provided libraries instead.

Some of the following libraries may be niche and generally not available as a system package. It is your job to ensure CMake find/configure modules are available for the libraries you choose to use. Good luck!
